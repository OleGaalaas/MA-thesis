KEY FINDINGS:

1. PERSON EXTRACTION ERRORS
   - Titles extracted as persons: 'Prime Minister', 'Deputy Director',
     'Criminal Code', 'Storm Shadow', 'Nobel Prize', etc. (~8-15% of conf=0 rows)
   - Historical figures: Napoleon, Archimedes, Van Gogh, Caravaggio
   - Worst stratum: conf0_Russia and conf0_other_foreign (~15% non-person)

2. UKRAINE WAR CONTAMINATION (conf0_Ukraine)
   - SEVERE systematic bias: articles about Ukraine cite Russian military experts
     who are then attributed to Ukraine via GPE proximity
   - Key repeat offenders: Yuri Knutov (12+ rows), Anatoly Matviychuk (10+ rows),
     Boris Yulin, Vladimir Evseev, Boris Rozhin, Alexei Zhivov
   - Also: Western analysts on Ukraine war (Mearsheimer, Barros, etc.) → Ukraine
   - ~50-60% of conf0_Ukraine country attributions are WRONG
   - country_final SHOULD correct repeat offenders via cross-article majority vote
     (e.g., Knutov appears hundreds of times as Russia elsewhere)
   - One-off foreign experts on Ukraine will NOT be corrected

3. OTHER FOREIGN ATTRIBUTION ERRORS
   - Russian experts attributed to foreign countries via GPE proximity:
     Dandykin → US, Timakov → UK, Surzhik → Japan, Byshok → Poland, etc.
   - These are conf=0 rows where topic GPE overrides expert's actual country

4. CONFIDENCE SCORE EFFECTIVENESS
   - conf=0 (unknown source): Highest error rate, especially Ukraine stratum
   - conf=0.55-0.65 (GPE): Moderate accuracy, direction matters
   - conf=0.7-0.8 (NORP, syntax_of_from): Good accuracy
   - conf≥0.85 (org_embedded, org_lookup, multi-source): Best performance
   - At conf≥0.85 threshold: ~94%+ person correct, ~86%+ country correct
   - EXCEPTION: Ukraine war contamination persists even at conf≥0.85
     (Anatoly Matviychuk, Oleg Glazunov, Konstantin Blokhin appear in conf_very_high
      stratum still attributed to Ukraine via syntax_of_from/GPE sources)
   - INTERPRETATION NOTE: For conf=0 strata, 'uncertain' rows cannot be individually
     confirmed from article text alone. Estimated true precision: ~85% for conf0_Russia,
     ~40-50% for conf0_Ukraine, ~70% for conf0_other_foreign.

5. RECALL (na_recall stratum)
   - Most articles correctly have no expert (pure news reporting)
   - Confirmed missed experts: ~13-15 cases in 250 articles
   - Recall miss rate: ~5-6% of na_recall articles had extractable experts
   - Types of misses: medical experts, energy analysts, regional specialists
   - No systematic pattern in recall misses (random NER failures)

6. SPORTS/OUT-OF-SCOPE EXPERTS
   - Football coaches, athletes appear (Paolo Vanoli, Guus Hiddink, etc.)
   - These are VALID extractions but out-of-scope for thesis
   - Topic modeling downstream will correctly remove these
   - Do NOT count as extraction errors

7. FOREIGN/DOMESTIC MISCLASSIFICATION
   - Primary error: Russian experts → Ukraine (foreign when should be domestic)
   - Secondary: topic-based GPE attribution creates spurious foreign experts
   - At conf≥0.85: foreign/domestic accuracy ~90%+
   - Below threshold: Ukraine stratum has ~50% f/d error rate
